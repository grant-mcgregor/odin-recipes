Deadly Burgers

The perfect guide to melt in your mouth burgers!

These burgers are going to be some of the best youll have ever tasted
and the best thing is theyre cheap to make and only require a few ingrdients!

ingrdients for 2 burgers

  • 250g - 80/20 Steak Mince
  • 2- Brioche burger buns
  • 4- Your choice of cheese slices i reccomend edam or cheddar
  • 6- Crinkle cut gherkins
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Recipe

  1. Form 125g of steak mince into a pattie x2
  2. Salt and pepper both sides of your patties
  3. Heat your pan to a high heat, solid bottom pans that are NOT non stick are best.
  4. Place your brioche bun in the toaster on a low setting 1-2, toast both
  5. Place your patties carefully in the pan.
  6. Wait until fat begins to pool on the surface of the burger then flip them.
  7. Cook the other side for roughly the same amount of time
  8. While the other side is cooking take your buns from the toaster and place them on a plate
  9. Give each top bun 5 squirts of ketchup and 5 squirts of mustard and place a slice of cheese on each bottom bun
  10. Place a slice of cheese on each base bun
  11. Take your burgers from the pan and place them on the bottom bun
  12. On top of each burger place another slice of cheese and then 3 gherkins
  13. Place the top bun on each and enjoy!

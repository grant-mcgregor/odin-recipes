Deadly Steak

The perfect guide to super tasty steaks!

These steeaks are going to be some of the best youll have ever tasted
and the best thing is theyre cheap to make and only require a few ingrdients!

ingrdients for 1 steak

  • 1 - ribeye
  • 1 - knob of butter
  • 2 stems of fresh rosemary
  • 3 - cloves of garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. Heat your pan to a high heat
  2. Salt and pepper both sides of your steak
  3. Place your knob of butter into the pan
  4. Upon the butter melting place your garlic in the pan
  5. Once the garlic is fragrant place the steak in the pan along with the rowsemary on top
  6. Tilt the pan so the butter rests in the edge and begin basting the steak with the butter
  7. Depending on your desired doneness wait till youre ready to flip the steak
  8. Cook the steak on the second side the same amount of time as the first.
  9. Plate the steak and wait 5 minutes before slicing into the steak
  10. Enjoy

Deadly Sandwich

The perfect guide to super tasty sandwiches!

These sandwiches are going to be some of the best youll have ever tasted
and the best thing is theyre cheap to make and only require a few ingrdients!

ingrdients for 1 sandwich

  • 4 slices - Pastrami
  • 2 slices - Bread - rye if possible
  • 4- cheese slices i reccomend mature cheddar
  • 8- Crinkle cut gherkins
  • Pepper
  • Mustard

Recipe

  1. Take both slices of bread and layer your choice of mustsrd over one side each
  2. Place a slice of mature chedder on both slices of bread
  3. Place a 2 slices of pastrami on both
  4. Pepper the pastrami and fit as many gherkins as you can on one slice.
  5. Lift the slice without the gherkins and plave it on top of the other
  6. Enjoy!
